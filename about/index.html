Welcome to Hriti's Game Hub! I built this collection of free online games with one goal in mind — to create a safe, fun, and ad-light place where kids can play without downloads, sign-ups, or hassle.

Every game here is crafted from scratch using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no engines, just pure code and creativity. From the icy waters of Penguin Paradise 🐧 to the far reaches of Space Blaster 🚀, each game is made with lots of ❤️ and attention to detail.

I'm always thinking about what to build next. Got an idea for a game? I'd love to hear it!
